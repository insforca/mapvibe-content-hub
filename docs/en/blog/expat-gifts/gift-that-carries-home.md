# The Gift That Carries Home

You know that feeling — someone you love moved abroad. A birthday coming up. A housewarming for their new flat. A Christmas far from home.

You want to give them something that says: *I know where you come from. I know what you miss.*

Not a generic city poster. Not a card. Something that holds the actual place.

---

## Why a Map Works When Nothing Else Does

A custom map of someone's hometown is different from any other gift because it's *specific in a way that takes effort*.

Anyone can buy a generic print of São Paulo. Getting someone a print of the *exact neighbourhood they grew up in*, at the exact zoom where their street is visible, in a style that matches the mood of the place — that's a different message entirely.

It says: I knew exactly where you were from. I looked it up. I made something just for you.

## How to Make the Gift

1. **Find their neighbourhood** — you probably know the city. Ask a mutual friend for the specific neighbourhood if you're unsure. Even the rough area is enough to start.
2. **Open [app.mapvibestudio.com](https://app.mapvibestudio.com)** and search the city
3. **Zoom in and drag** to centre on their neighbourhood
4. **Try a few bearings** — 30° or 45° often looks more interesting than flat north-up
5. **Run through the themes** — pick the one that feels right for the place and the person
6. **Order and ship** — we ship to US and Canada

::: tip
If you're gifting to a Brazilian expat: São Paulo's Vila Madalena, Pinheiros, or Lapa at 30° bearing in Mediterranean Vibes is one of the most beautiful combinations we've seen. Rio's Santa Teresa or Lapa at any bearing in Vintage Noir is equally striking.
:::
