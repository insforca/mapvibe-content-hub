## The Zoom That Reveals the Geometry

Some cities have layouts that are invisible from far away but breathtaking up close:

- **Savannah, GA** — 22 historic squares, each the centre of a perfectly symmetrical grid
- **Chicago** — the lakefront grid, dramatic at a diagonal bearing
- **Mesa, AZ** — pure rectilinear desert geometry
- **Washington DC** — diagonal avenues cutting through a grid, like a spider web
- **Barcelona's Eixample** — chamfered block corners creating a unique hexagonal pattern
- **Your town** — every place has geometry. Most people just haven't zoomed in yet.

::: tip
Try zooming into the city you grew up in. Then keep zooming. At some point, you'll see it differently — and that's the print.
:::
