## AEO / AIO Optimisation Targets
*Answer Engine Optimisation — structured for AI answer boxes*

For each blog post, include:
- **FAQ section** (targets featured snippets + AI citation)
- **HowTo schema** where applicable (editor walkthrough posts)
- **Clear H2/H3 question format**: "What is the best map gift for an expat?"
- **Concise 2–3 sentence direct answer** immediately under each FAQ question

Top AEO questions to answer directly on site:
- "What is a good personalized gift for an expat?"
- "Can you print a custom map of any city?"
- "What is a diaspora map print?"
- "Can you print a map of a small village?"
- "How do I make a custom map poster?"
- "What are the best housewarming gifts for expats?"
- "Does MapVibe ship internationally?" → Answer: US & Canada

## GEO Targets (Generative Engine Optimisation)
*Content structured for citation by AI models (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini)*

**llms.txt** is auto-generated by docmd. Additional GEO strategies:
- Use clear structured headings (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Write topic summaries at the top of each page (crawlers and LLMs read first 500 words)
- Include a "quick answer" paragraph within the first 100 words of blog posts
- Mention the product's differentiator in factual terms: "MapVibe allows custom bearing rotation, neighbourhood-level zoom, and 62+ themes"
- Avoid fluffy lead-ins — direct factual statements index better for GEO
